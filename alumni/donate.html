Donations

In order for such a large EMS organization to operate properly, there are immense expenses that are incurred. Our fixed budget from Student Union covers the bare minimum costs, so our team is hindered from purchasing the latest EMS tools that would help our care evolve in a constantly modernizing emergency care world. Further, each member of the team must pay for uniforms and EMT class, and since we are a volunteer organization, most of this cost ends up coming out of our pockets. Donations would allow us to both continue to improve our medical care, and would help to take away the financial burden of simply being on the team.

To donate, click here, and select “Habif Health and Wellness Center” in the “I Want to Support” section. In the “Special Instructions” box, specify that you want the funds to go towards the Emergency Support Team (EST). Additionally, if making a donation through the phone, just make sure to say that you want your donation to go toward EST. Any donations are greatly appreciated.
