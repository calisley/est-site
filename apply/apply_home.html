Apply to Join

Every fall semester EST selects a new tech class. Being a part of EST offers an opportunity to serve your community, learn life-saving medical skill,s and be a part of a tight-knit student organization. Members of EST graduate from Washington University with unique and invaluable knowledge, expierences, and friendships.

To become a member of EST, you must:

  • Be enrolled as an undergraduate student at Washington University in Freshman or Sophomore standing
  • Be physically capable of performing all skills and functions required of an EMT-B
  • Participate in the annual fall application process

The application process for our ner tech class begins late August after classes have begun. Final decisions are made mid-October. The process includes a written application and up to three rounds of intervirews For reasons of resoucrce limitation, both finance - and personel related- EST is capable of selecting only 14 and 18 members per year. On average, approximately 110 students apply to EST each year.
