Other Medical Services

EST is but one medical function serving the Danforth campus. The following organizations work with EST to keep Washington University safe and healthy.

Student Health Services (SHS) – located right next to Dardick House on the South 40, SHS offers both appointment and walk-in visits. SHS provides both medical and mental health services. During their daytime hours, they are your best choice for evaluation and treatment of non-emergent medical illnesses. When SHS is closed, students can call a nurse to ask any medical questions at (314) 935-6666. For more information about their hours and services, visit their website. SHS also offers information for survivors and friends of survivors of sexual assault at coping.wustl.edu, and a suicide prevention training program at asklistenrefer.org/washu.

Sexual Assault and Rape Anonymous Helpline (SARAH) – a student-run helpline specialized in rape, sexual assault, abuse, and relationships. SARAH is a completely confidential service available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Call SARAH at (314) 935-8080. For more information, visit their website.

Uncle Joe's Peer Counseling - a student-run confidential counseling service that provides both phone and walk-in services. For more information, visit their website.
