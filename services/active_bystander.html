Active Bystander Training

EST is excited to announce that we offer Active Bystander Trainings for anybody (student group, organization, residential college, etc) at Washington University in St. Louis. These trainings are completely free of cost.

Our Active Bystander Training is a 30 minute interactive presentation regarding how to be an active bystander for someone who is too intoxicated. We discuss symptoms of a severely intoxicated individual and what to do/what not to do for that person. We also talk about other injuries or conditions that can present itself with similar symptoms to intoxication, and what to do in those scenarios.
