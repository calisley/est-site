CPR Classes

EST is incredibly excited to announce that we now offer American Red Cross Adult & Pediatric CPR/AED/First Aid Classes for all Washington University students and faculty. We look forward to this opportunity to better serve our community.

The courses will be held 9:30 AM-12:30PM (CPR/AED) and 1-2:30 PM (optional First Aid portion) on the following Saturdays of the Spring 2019 semester:

  • January 27th
  • February 17th
  • March 24th
  • April 21st

While these classes are offered by Red Cross for $70-$90, we will be offering classes for $25 for CPR/AED and $35 for CPR/AED/First Aid. Class size will be limited to 20 people, on a first come first serve basis. The classes will all be held in Seigle Hall.

Please fill out the following Google form if you are interested in completing one of our courses, and contact us at estcpr@gmail.com if you have any questions. Please try to sign up at least one week in advance and give one week's notice if you will not be able to attend, so that we are able to offer those spots to others who may be interested. We will send all participants signed up an email by the Tuesday before each class with details about the course (room number, what to bring, etc.)

Note: Departments or student groups may contact estcpr@gmail.com for information about group trainings. Due to limited class size, we may not be able to accommodate very large groups in a single class.
